The FireTest Company Advantage

On Time Inspections:

To avoid overdue inspections, we utilize our inspection scheduling database to keep track of when your inspection is due. We will contact you prior to the due date to schedule.

Technicians:

We send the same technician team for every inspection, so that once they are familiar with your facility, they will save your staff time and build a level of trust and efficiency.

Discrepancies and Repair Management:

We provide a detailed discrepancy list and proposal to repair it. Once the repair is made, we provide a work order showing the details of the repair matching the initial inspection report. The work order is available to you online 24 hours a day, 360 days a year, and can be viewed online, downloaded and printed, and/or emailed.

Inspection Reports:

Your reports are available online 24 hours a day, 360 days a year, and can be viewed online, downloaded and printed, and/or emailed.

Parts and Programming:

We believe that maintaining your system's warranty and integrity is valuable and must be protected. We encourage you to buy parts and programming from factory authorized technicians. Because we are not a product distributor and do not pose competition to the manufacture's distributors, we maintain relationships with manufacture distributors, making it easy for you to get parts and programming from your preferred distributor or one of ours. We have access to most all manufacturer's parts new and old.

Pricing:

We are extremely competitive because of our low overhead and efficiency through modern technology. All of our reports, time sheets, work orders, scheduling, and communication, are managed through wireless devices in the cloud, so that technician and office time is automated and in real time. This eliminates hours spent on time sheets, work orders, and reports.

Knowledge and Experience:

We are licensed with the State of Colorado, local jurisdictions and are NICET certified. Our team leader worked for a major hospital for over 9 years and has over 27 years of experience in Life Safety inspections, testing and maintenance methods, record keeping and binder management for JCAHO, CMS, risk management, safety management, insurance and fire authorities.
